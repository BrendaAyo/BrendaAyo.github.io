Geospatial Analysis on Student physical activeness


Working alongside Senait Meles (Institut f√ºr Geoinformatik, WWU), our final course project in Tracking the Trackers focused on demonstrating the use of Global Navigation Satellite Systems (e.g. GPS) and Geographical Information Systems (GIS) measure, analyze and map the Physical Activity in students and how they interact with green spaces and the built environment around them.


We recruited 5 Students (2 Males, 3 Females) and collected 5 Days Data using the GeoTracker mobile App and a BT-Q1000XT GPS Tracker. We mapped were able to collect data on Streets, Parks and Recreation, Home, School and Transportation Mode. Spatial analysis was carried out in ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Insights to assess where each student spends most time and their interactions with green spaces.
