Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR)- Differential SAR Interferometry


In this tutorial, I take you through the step-by-step instructions on two-pass Differential SAR Interferometry (DInSAR) with Sentinel-1 imagery using the ESA SNAP software. The application of satellite DInSAR differential synthetic aperture radar (SAR) interferometry include mapping, monitoring and analyzing groundwater flow, Land Deformation measurement and Monitoring etc.
