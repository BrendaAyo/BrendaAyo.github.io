Correlation Between an Eagle Owl’s Flight Height and Temperature


The GitHub repo with the Scripts and ArcGIS Toolboxes are available HERE.


In this Python in GIS Class Project, Maicol Camargo, Gulraiz Khan, Ignacio Ponsoda, Mutaz Qafisheh and I demonstrated how Python in GIS with emphasis on Arcpy and open source python libraries like Numpy, Matplotlib and Pandas can be used to answer the research question “What is the correlation between monthly mean temperature variation and movement behavior male and female eagle owls in terms of flight height and how can we seek any visible pattern between the variations of these two variables?”.


This was based on GPS Tracking data of 21 eagle owls was obtained from the Movebank online database of animal tracking. The 21 eagle owls’ data is located in Germany states of Nordrhein-Westfalen, Bayern, Hessen and Niedersachsen with most of the data located in Nordrhein-Westfalen. ArcGIS Toolboxes for Data preprocessing, Analysis and Post processing were generated to automate the workflow and to ensure reproducibility on similar data.
